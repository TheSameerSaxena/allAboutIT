eCLERX

ABOUT THE COMPANY

eClerx Services Limited, founded in 2000 and publicly listed on both the Bombay Stock Exchange and National Stock Exchange in India since 2007, is a leading knowledge process outsourcing (KPO) company providing middle and back office operations support to over 30 Fortune 500 companies.
Headquartered in Mumbai, our delivery centers across India, Italy, and Thailand support a diverse global client base, including the world’s leading Financial Services, Broadband, Cable and Telecom, eCommerce and Retail, High Tech, Industrial Manufacturing and Distribution, Software, Media and Entertainment, and Travel companies.
Our annual revenue in FY 16 was USD 200 million.
eClerx’s three business verticals include Financial Services, Digital Services, and Cable and Telecom Services.
Our clients rely on us for innovative, effective, highly-accurate, and cost-efficient operation of their critical business processes.
A scalable, 24x7x365 domain-centric delivery model is at the core of our success.

eCLERX RECRUITMENT PROCESS

In this company, you have openings for various domains. The interview process is same for almost all these positions. The interview process at eClerx is consisting of 4 rounds. They are:
Step 1: Online Written exam
Step 2: GD or JAM (optional)
Step 3: Technical Interview
Step 4: HR Interview
