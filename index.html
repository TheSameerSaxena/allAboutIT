What is allAboutIT?


At allAboutIT, our sole purpose is to help students of MIT-WPU find a job and company that are affiliated with MIT-WPU.


Focusing mainly on students/freshers, our company directory offers vast options of the latest job listings, combined with a growing database of company reviews, interview experiences, Recruitment process, FAQs and many more to help them find the job details of the company they are interested in.


Job seekers on allAboutIT get informed about the jobs and companies they apply to and consider joining.
