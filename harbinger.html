HARBINGER GROUP

ABOUT THE COMPANY

Harbinger Group consists of Harbinger Systems, Harbinger Interactive Learning and Harbinger Knowledge Products.
Harbinger Systems is a global company providing software tech services for independent software vendors and enterprises, with a specialization in product engineering.
They build software solutions leveraging social, mobile, analytics, cloud technologies and IoT for domains such as Human Capital Management, healthcare, e-Learning and publishing.
They specialize in providing end-to-end services for software products and are often 100% of the engineering strength of their clients.
Mission: Serving Global Customers with our Award Winning Software Products and Services.
Our Vision: Transforming lives at every workplace. Our Values: Customer centricity, Developing People, Agility, Meritocracy, Inclusvity and Professionalism.
